About

"It's not wise to violate rules until you know how to observe them."

At Sharp Cuts, we take pride in providing luxury quality,yet affordable men's hair and grooming services, within a relaxed, yet stylish environment. We place emphasis on precision and attention to detail, not only during our hair cuts but from the moment you walk through our door.

Our Master Barbers are expertly trained to provide a range of services featuring our products and pure essential oils to leave clients well-groomed and relaxed.

As modern gentlemen we understand that looking good is important - and can impact on how we feel about ourselves and how we are perceived by others. We also know that busy lifestyles don't often allow much opportunity to kick back and unwind.

You may just find, once you've arrived on our quiet cobbled street in Bourbon Loop, chilled out in our cozy waiting area with a selection of complimentary refreshments, listening to our smooth background music while flicking through sports magazines, that perhaps Sharp Barber Spa could become part of your regular routine.
